Last time we played High Wycombe - Round 2, 2019

Last time we played High Wycombe, we lost by a small margin. I slotted 3 goals and had a number of crucial disposals, however I was subbed off through a calf injury. It was the second game of the season, and our first at home.

Predicted line-up

The line-up will be somewhat similar to our match against the Mandurah Mustangs, as that was the last game that we played with our full-strength team (because of state championships).
