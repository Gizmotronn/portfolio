Orgzy Design

Orgzy Design is an Australian website and game development organisation that is pushing the limits of technology. I founded Orgzy in 2019 and since then we have created a number of games that are almost ready to be released, including Asteroid Dodgers, Pong 2019 and Star Sailors, a game tie-in with the Star Sailors novella written by ACORD Robotics.

Orgzy works primarily with small companies, however we also have a partnership with ACORD Robotics
