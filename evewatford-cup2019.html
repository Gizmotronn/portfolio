The heat's off Marco Silva...for another week!

by Liam Arbuckle

The sun is smiling at Goodison Park today, with a win over Watford in the Round of 16 giving Everton a quarter final birth in the EFL Cup for the second time in 12 years. With goals from Mason Holgate (Header), who scored his first goal for the Blues in 54 games, and from Richarlison (Shot), Everton won 2-0, but not without a scare.
