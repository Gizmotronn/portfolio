If Everton got relegated, who would stay?

by Liam Arbuckle

I’d say players mostly past their prime. Siggy (Sigurddson, while he is my favourite player, is getting old) would fit into that category. He is a great attacking mid and has done some great work for us, but I’m not sure which clubs would be interested in him after the season he’s had so far in 2019/20. I know it’s not his fault, that the manager (Silva) is playing him and the team in an annoying 4.2.3.1 formation, but not many clubs would be willing to shell out for him, and he would probably know that it was an anomaly and we would probably get promoted again anyway. Pickford would go, he’s England’s keeper and would get out of that shit as soon as possible. Richarlison would go, we wouldn’t get much back for him and while he’s a good player, he smacks of disloyalty - when he first moved here he said how he wanted to be noticed by a big club like Barca and that would be his ticket out of Goodison. Digne might leave. Gomes & Bernard would likely stay, as they seem to think that we are building towards something great (I do too, maybe a cup run as well as a good league finish). Bernard isn’t getting a game in that #20 position right now so I think he would jump at the chance to play some first team football, and besides we’re a big club so even if we were relegated it would most likely be the best football he could get.

In short: I don’t know. Cenk Tosun may leave. Schneirderlin would be sold. He’s good, but does NOT fit into our lineup. He uses too much of our wage bill. The younger players that haven’t broken into the first team, like Davies, would most likely stay. But hopefully it won’t happen, and if it does Everton won’t go down without a fight.
